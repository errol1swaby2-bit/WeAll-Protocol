# Token marketing guardrails

Status: non-lawyer draft, pending counsel review.

WeCoin may be described only as a locked/testnet-safe protocol accounting design until future governance, legal, economic simulation, and public testnet gates are complete.

Do not describe WeCoin as:

- an investment;
- a promise of profit;
- yield;
- a public sale;
- a live transferable asset;
- a payment service;
- a securities-law conclusion.

Safe phrasing:

- "locked tokenomics implementation";
- "epoch-based issuance policy under test";
- "economics activation remains gated";
- "civic/social/governance actions remain fee-free".
